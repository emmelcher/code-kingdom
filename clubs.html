Clubs

  • Academic Challenge
    • Academic Challenge is a forum for students to compete against other Berks County Schools in the areas of Math, Science, Literature, Social Studies, Arts, and Current Events. Structured somewhat like Jeopardy, students compete on teams against one or two other schools at a time. Scores and records are kept based on wins and losses, and the top teams can even move on and compete at a Regional and State level. Practices are usually held once a week and the competitions are once a month in November, December, January, and February.
